How it works

English Audio Generator uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis engine — no servers, no uploads, no sign-up. Your text never leaves your device. Click record, share the tab audio when prompted, and download the resulting file directly to your computer.

The downloaded file (MP3 format) is compatible with PowerPoint, Google Slides, and any media player or video editing software. It's perfect for creating listening exercises, dictation tracks, pronunciation models, and narrated presentations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool completely free?

Yes. It uses your browser's built-in text-to-speech engine, which is free and requires no API keys or subscriptions. There are no hidden costs.

Which browsers are supported?

Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge work best for recording and downloading. The text-to-speech preview works in all modern browsers, but audio download requires Chrome or Edge due to the tab audio capture feature.

Can I use the audio in PowerPoint?

Yes. After downloading, go to Insert → Audio → Audio on my PC and select the MP3 file. MP3 is supported in all versions of PowerPoint.

What accents are available?

Available voices depend on your operating system. Chrome on Windows typically includes US English, UK English, and others. You can select from all installed English voices in the Voice dropdown.

Is my text stored or sent anywhere?

No. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.
